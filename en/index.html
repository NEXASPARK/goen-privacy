Privacy Policy

goen ／ NEXASPARK, Inc.
Effective: June 12, 2026　Last updated: June 12, 2026

NEXASPARK, Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") establishes this Privacy Policy (this "Policy") regarding the handling of users' information in the mobile app "goen" (the "Service") that we provide.

1. Basic Principles

The Service is designed at its core to deliver the experience of "connecting on the spot with people who happen to be nearby" using the minimum information necessary. As a rule, we do not collect information that directly identifies an individual (name, phone number, email address) or precise location coordinates.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Name, phone number, email address: When you use "Sign in with Apple," we do not request your name or email.
  • Precise location coordinates (latitude/longitude): Only a value rounded on your device to a coarse cell (a geohash, roughly 150m square) is sent. Precise coordinates are never sent off the device or stored.
  • Message content: Messages are end-to-end encrypted between devices. We cannot decrypt or view the content on our servers or in transit.
  • Tracking for advertising or behavioral profiling: We do not use third-party advertising SDKs or any mechanism that tracks users across apps.

4. Handling of Location Information

  • Location is rounded on your device to a coarse cell (a geohash) before being sent; precise coordinates are not sent.
  • Presence information expires automatically after a set time (the "here now" display lasts about 5 minutes; the last position shown on the map lasts up to about 30 days). If reporting stops, it is deleted automatically.
  • We do not create or store any individual movement history (route).
  • Background location use (optional): If you turn on "When someone with goen is nearby" in notification settings, the app uses location in the background to notify you when you enter an area with people, even when the app is not open (the OS "Always" permission is required). Even in the background, only a geohash (coarse cell) is sent — never your exact coordinates. This is optional and can be turned off anytime in the app's settings or your OS settings.
  • You can stop the use of location information at any time from your OS settings.

5. Handling of Bluetooth (Proximity Feature)

  • The proximity feature operates only while the app is open and exchanges a rotating temporary ID and a profile card with nearby devices. The advertisement does not include personal information such as your name.
  • Turning Bluetooth off via the in-app toggle or your OS settings stops all detection, being detected, and exchange.

6. Message Encryption

Messages are encrypted on the sending device with the recipient's public key and can be decrypted only on the recipient's device. Our servers store only ciphertext and cannot read the content. Because encryption keys are stored on your device, reinstalling the app or similar actions may make past messages impossible to decrypt.

7. Provision to Third Parties and Subcontracting

Except as required by law and similar cases, we do not sell or provide collected information to third parties. To operate the Service, we entrust the storage and processing of data to the following cloud services.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Presence information expires automatically after a set time, as described above.
  • Running "Delete Account" in the app deletes related data such as your profile, Goen, conversations, messages, and blocks.
  • Goen requests that are not connected disappear automatically by the end of the day.

9. Safety and Age Restriction

The Service is available only to those aged 18 or older. Users can block other users or report abusive behavior. We review reported content and take measures such as suspending accounts as needed.

10. Your Rights

You may request the disclosure, correction, suspension of use, deletion, and so on of your own information that we hold. In addition to the actions available in the app (editing your profile, deleting your account, etc.), please contact us at the address below.

11. Changes to This Policy
